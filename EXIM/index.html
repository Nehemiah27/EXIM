About Us

Nehemiah's Global India Exports & Imports, an established Freight Forwarder, Licensed Customs House Agent and Project Cargo handling with presence in 46 Countries covering 12 Cities across the world through our network. Our commitment to customers, attention to detail, correct guidance and tailor made service is our hallmark. With a combined expertise of over 100 years, we offer tailor made solutions and introduce simple processes to ease the way International Business is done within the complicated confines, rules and regulations that covers EXIM TRADE in & out of India.

With a combined experience of over a hundred years, we bring to you technical, legal, financial and logistics expertise helping our customers in realizing their TOTAL EXIM SOLUTIONS in all aspects of the trade. We have been growing fundamentally in an organic manner over the years starting with a turnover of Rs. 20,000 to now having Offices in 7 cities within a short span of 15 years.

Starting with our own offices at Chennai as Head Office, we have added extended our own office network to Aurangabad Nasik, Mumbai & Vapi across Western India and Agency agreement offices on PAN India basis to ensure smooth handling of our customer’s business. We always strive to ensure best industry standards & practices are applied while handling any scope of work in relation to EXIM Trade in India & abroad.
